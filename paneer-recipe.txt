Ingredients:
 - 2 cup water
 - 200g paneer(Cheese)
 - 2 spoons of vegitable mix chilli powder
 - 2 spoons of salt and other sour powder
 - 50g mattar(peas) uncooked
 - 200g of cutted potatoe

Directions:
  1. Boil potatoe in water for 15 mins, mix vegetable powder, salt 
  2. After frying paneer for 10 minutes, mix it to boiling potatoe water
  3. Stir the whole solution for another 10 minutes.
